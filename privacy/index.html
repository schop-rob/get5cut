What data 5cut processes

5cut processes your selected videos and audio locally on your device to create a trimmed output video. Your media is not uploaded to a 5cut server.

Information collected

5cut does not require an account and does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, contacts, or precise location.

The app stores some information locally on your device to function correctly:

  • App settings (e.g., default processing settings, theme)
  • Export limit state (free vs. premium usage counters)

Permissions

Photos (Add Only): Used to save exported videos to your photo library. The app requests "Add Only" access and does not need to read your entire library.

Files: Used when you import a video file via the file picker.

Purchases

If you purchase Premium (via monthly subscription or lifetime purchase), StoreKit and the App Store handle the transaction. 5cut does not receive your payment details. Apple may provide us with non-identifying purchase status information needed to unlock Premium features.

Analytics & tracking

5cut does not currently send analytics or tracking data to third-party servers. If this changes, this policy will be updated and you will be informed in the app.

Your rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g., GDPR in the EU), you may have rights to access, correct, or delete personal data. Since 5cut does not maintain user accounts or store your media on our servers, most data stays on your device.

For questions, contact us at support@get5cut.com.
