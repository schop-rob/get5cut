Remove Silence from Podcast Episodes

Dead air kills pacing. Whether you're editing your own podcast or listening to one, those 2–3 second pauses between sentences and topic changes add up fast. A 45-minute episode might have 8–10 minutes of silence.

5cut removes it in one tap.

For podcast creators

You've recorded an episode. The content is great, but the pacing is off — too many pauses where you were thinking, checking notes, or waiting for your co-host. Normally you'd scrub through the whole timeline in a DAW. With 5cut:

  1. Import your recording
  2. 5cut shows the waveform — green is speech, red is silence
  3. Pick Gentle mode (keeps natural pauses) or Moderate (tighter pacing)
  4. Export and upload

For podcast listeners

Some podcasts are great content wrapped in terrible pacing. If you're binge-listening or studying from podcast-format lectures, 5cut can tighten episodes before you add them to your playlist.

Speaker identification

Interview format? Panel discussion? 5cut identifies up to 4 speakers automatically with color-coded labels. Rename them for easy reference in the transcript.

Generate transcripts

Transcribe your episode in 30+ languages directly on-device. Export as SRT for show notes, accessibility, or SEO. No cloud service needed.

Smart Mode presets

  • Gentle — keeps conversational pauses for natural rhythm
  • Moderate — balanced pacing, recommended for most podcasts
  • Aggressive — maximum tightening for information-dense content

Batch processing

Editing a series? Queue up multiple episodes and process them all with Premium batch mode. Processing continues in the background.

Privacy and pricing

All processing is on-device. Free with 10 exports/month. Premium: $1.99/month or $6.99 lifetime.
