Remove Silence from OBS Recordings

Edit your Twitch VODs and OBS streams in minutes, not hours.

Frequently asked questions

Does 5cut support OBS recordings?

Yes, 5cut fully supports MP4 recordings exported from OBS Studio. If you record in MKV, simply remux to MP4 within OBS before transferring to your iPhone or iPad.

Will silence removal desync my game audio?

No, 5cut trims the entire video and audio timeline simultaneously, ensuring perfect lip-sync and game audio synchronization.
