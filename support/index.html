Frequently Asked Questions

How do I import a video?

Tap the + button on the home screen and select a video from your photo library or files.

How does silence detection work?

5cut analyzes the audio track of your video on-device to find segments below the silence threshold. You can adjust the threshold and minimum silence duration in the editor settings.

Can I undo cuts or adjust the result?

Yes. Use the threshold slider to change how aggressively silence is detected. Your original video is never modified.

What does Premium unlock?

Premium is a one-time purchase that removes the export limit and watermark. You get unlimited exports for all your videos, forever.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open 5cut, go to Settings, and tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

Is my video uploaded anywhere?

No. All processing happens locally on your device. Your videos never leave your phone.

The export is shorter than expected

Make sure you're using the latest version of 5cut. If the issue persists, try adjusting the silence threshold to a lower value and re-exporting.

How do I request a refund?

Purchases are processed by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and select the purchase.
